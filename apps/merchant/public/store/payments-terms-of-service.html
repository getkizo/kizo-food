Payment Terms of Service

Last updated: March 2026

These Payment Terms of Service govern the processing of payments made through our online ordering platform. By placing an order and proceeding to payment, you agree to the terms below.

1. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely by Finix, a licensed payment facilitator. We do not store your full card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment credentials on our servers. Payment data is transmitted directly to Finix over an encrypted (TLS) connection.

2. Accepted Payment Methods

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Debit cards with a Visa or Mastercard logo

3. Order Total & Charges

The total amount charged to your card includes the subtotal of your selected items, applicable sales tax, and any tip you choose to add. The exact amount is displayed on the payment page before you confirm your order.

4. Authorization & Capture

When you submit a payment, your card is charged immediately. You will receive a confirmation of your order on-screen. If the transaction is declined by your card issuer, your order will not be placed and no charge will be applied.

5. Refunds & Cancellations

Refund eligibility is determined at the merchant's discretion and in accordance with applicable law. If a refund is approved, it will be returned to the original payment method. Processing times vary by card issuer but are typically 3–7 business days.

Scheduled orders may be cancelled free of charge before the cancellation window closes (displayed at the time of ordering). Orders that have entered preparation cannot be cancelled or refunded.

6. Chargebacks

If you believe an unauthorised transaction has occurred, please contact your card issuer directly. For disputes about an order you placed, please contact us first — most issues can be resolved quickly without initiating a chargeback.

7. Data & Privacy

Payment information is handled by Finix in accordance with Finix's Privacy Policy and PCI DSS requirements. Your name and contact details provided at checkout are used solely to fulfil your order and send status notifications.

8. Contact

For payment-related questions or to request a refund, please speak to a staff member in person or contact the restaurant directly.
